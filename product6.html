Description: The CORSAIR iCUE H170i ELITE LCD XT Liquid CPU Cooler delivers powerful, customized cooling for your CPU, with a personalized LCD screen that displays GIFs, images, and system information.
PREMIUM ALL-IN-ONE LCD CPU COOLER
Combines powerful, low-noise cooling with a personalized LCD display to accent your system's aesthetics and track its real-time performance at a glance.


YOUR OWN PERSONALIZED LCD DASHBOARD
Upload your own animated GIFs and images or choose from numerous pre-installed screens and lighting profiles for groundbreaking system lighting and customization options.


BRILLIANT IPS LCD DISPLAY
Showcase your favorite animated GIF, funniest meme, your team's logo, or anything you like on a custom 2.1" IPS LCD screen with 480x480 resolution and ultra-bright 600cd/m backlight.


HIGH-PERFORMANCE PUMP HEAD WITH RGB LED RING
Don't sacrifice performance to thermal throttling. A powerful, low-noise pump head ensures you can squeeze every last MHz out of compatible CPUs.


UPGRADE EVERYTHING
Each ELITE LCD XT Cooler comes with an iCUE COMMANDER CORE that can control lighting and fan speeds of not only the included fans, but up to a total of six.
xAdditional fans sold separately.

OUR BEST PERFORMING RGB FANS
CORSAIR AF RGB ELITE PWM fans with AirGuide technology deliver massive low-noise airflow along a high-surface area 240, 360, or 420mm radiator for extreme CPU cooling performance.


EXTREME PERFORMANCE THERMAL PASTE
All ELITE LCD XT AIOs include pre-applied CORSAIR XTM70 Extreme Performance thermal paste for maximum heat transfer from your high-end processor to the cooler cold plate.
For reliably high cooling performance, every step in the heat transfer and dissipation chain matters.



ZERO RPM MODE
Dictate your exact fan speeds with PWM control, plus support for 0 RPM PWM signals that allow fans to stop entirely at low temperatures, eliminating fan noise completely.


EXTENDED TUBING AND EASY INSTALLATION
Extended tubing length of 400mm (H100i) / 450mm (H150i, H170i) and modular, tool-free mounting brackets make for easy installation on the latest Intel and AMD motherboard sockets, including LGA 115X, 1200, 1700, 2011, and 2066, plus AMD Socket AM4, AM5, and sTRX4/sTR4.
